About us

    We are a non-profit organization dedicated to the open-source industry. The industry blocked by commerce has blocked the world that is gradually open source and open. Here we will step by step unlock the yoke of commerce, accelerate the development of science and technology, help the progress of civilization, and let knowledge be shared with all mankind.

    Open source industry refers to open source software and hardware that can be applied to industrial production. We hope to help the majority of SMEs to improve production efficiency at the lowest cost, so as to benefit the whole society. For individuals, we will help everyone, from a simple mechanical painter, from a pastor to copy and copy, grow step by step into a true mechanical engineer, programming engineer.

    In today's society, the ever-changing appearance cannot hide the stagnation of scientific progress. The growth of wealth cannot stop the polarization of the rich and the poor. The rise of artificial intelligence has transformed us from a soulful person into a source of data creation step by step. Only by constantly learning, making progress, and sharing can we avoid a gap in the shady front and let the glory of prosperity spread to the earth.
